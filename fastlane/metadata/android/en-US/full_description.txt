<p><i>osmin</i> is a GPS navigator On-Road/Off-Road for Android (and Sailfish) which is strongly inspired by <a href='https://github.com/Karry/osmscout-sailfish' target='_blank' rel='nofollow'>osmscout</a> by Karry, it uses a fork of <a href='https://github.com/Karry/libosmscout' target='_blank' rel='nofollow'>libosmscout</a> as backend. It includes features such as generic compass, tracker, GPX reader/writer, road router, POI database.</p><p>Routing with your Android device is supported with Android OS Nougat (Android 7.0) or newer, LineageOS 14.1 or newer.</p>
